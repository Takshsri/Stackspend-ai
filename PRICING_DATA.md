# PRICING_DATA.md — StackSpend AI

## Purpose

This document tracks the pricing sources used by the audit engine.

Every pricing recommendation generated by `audit-engine.ts` must map to:
- a publicly verifiable vendor pricing page
- a known pricing tier
- a manually verified price

This prevents hallucinated pricing recommendations and keeps the audit engine deterministic and explainable.

# Pricing Philosophy

## 1. Deterministic Recommendations
All audit recommendations are rule-based and traceable to real vendor pricing pages.

The AI summary system never invents pricing numbers.

---

## 2. Value-Based Pricing
If StackSpend AI identifies hundreds or thousands of dollars in annual savings, a low monthly subscription becomes easy to justify.

---

## 3. Free Tier Drives Virality
Public shareable audit reports are designed to:
- spread organically
- generate referrals
- reduce CAC
- demonstrate product value instantly

---

## 4. Simplicity Over Enterprise Complexity
The product intentionally avoids:
- procurement-heavy workflows
- enterprise onboarding friction
- configuration-heavy dashboards

The goal is fast AI spend visibility for SMBs and developer teams.

---

# Audit Engine Validation

The audit engine uses deterministic pricing logic stored in:
- `audit-engine.ts`
- `pricing-config.ts`

No AI-generated pricing recommendations are used.

Reason:
financial recommendations must be explainable, auditable, and reproducible.

Incorrect pricing hallucinations would reduce trust in the audit system.
