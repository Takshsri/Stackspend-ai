## Competitor Pricing Research

### Enterprise Tools (Not our competition, but useful for context)

| Tool | Price | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Zylo | ~$30,000+/yr | Enterprise (1000+ employees) | Full SAM platform, requires sales call |
| Torii | ~$15,000–50,000/yr | Mid-market to enterprise | Discovery + workflow automation |
| Cleanshelf | ~$20,000/yr | Enterprise | License optimisation focus |
| Productiv | Custom | Large enterprise | Usage analytics |
| BetterCloud | Custom | IT/SaaS ops | More IT management than spend |

**Insight:** No major player under $500/mo for SMBs. Giant gap at $0–$200/mo.

### Lightweight / Adjacent Tools

| Tool | Price | Gap |
|---|---|---|
| Cledara | $99–499/mo | Mostly a purchasing/billing tool, not an audit tool |
| Spendesk | Custom | Expense management, not SaaS audit |
| Ramp (software module) | Free (with Ramp card) | Only if you use Ramp for spend; limited insights |
| Vendr | Custom | Procurement negotiation, not DIY |

**Insight:** Even the "lightweight" options are either expensive or require switching your whole payment workflow.

## Our Pricing Philosophy

1. **Price on value, not cost** — If we surface $500 in savings, $29/mo is trivial
2. **Free tier drives virality** — Shareable audit results = word of mouth
3. **Don't compete on features with enterprise** — Compete on speed, simplicity, price
4. **Raise prices as AI quality improves** — Better recommendations = more defensible pricing
